The Saiba World

Get in Early

Enter Saiba is a 777 limited pieces edition NFT collection that offers investment opportunities to holders in the short, medium, and long term. They will have early access to all our additional projects in the future.

About

The Project

Our Vision

Imagine a future where the NFT market is organized on a safe platform, built on a structured system free of scams, and has a unique filtering method that is loyal to the blockchain, works on the web 3.0 and helps to attract new users to the market.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a community platform, where only those teams can start building communities that have been substantially reviewed and approved by our team. We track the roadmap of each project and if the community deems it necessary we hold the creators to account for their actions.

The Beginnings

We want the Saiba Platform to start from the core of the NFT community. With this method, our team can make profits and others who will follow us along the way and see our vision. We realize this idea in 4 phases.

Phase 01

Enter Saiba

Launch on a Launchpad

The project will be launched on an official launchpad website.

Listing on Magic Eden

We’ll be listing the Enter Saiba collection on Solana’s best-known secondary marketplace. We provide this to allow our customers to trade early.

Royalty Distribution

77% of royalties will be distributed among the owners of Enter Saiba every month.

Free NFT in Phase 2 (10K)

As long as they do NOT list their NFTs on the marketplace on the date we have set, Enter Saiba buyers will be entitled to one free NFT per wallet from the Phase 2 collection.

Instant Whitelist in Phase 2

As long as they do NOT list their NFTs on the marketplace on the date we have set, Enter Saiba buyers will be entitled to a free WL slot per wallet in the Phase 2 collection.

Staking

We offer an opportunity to stake to owners after the closing of Phase 2.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Why Solana?

Due to the high gas fees on the Ethereum chain, we opted for the Solana BlockChain.

Is there a Whitelist?

Yes. There will be a whitelist for members who have been active in our community and showed dedication to the project and future of the Saiba.

How can I get Whitelisted?

Please join our Discord where the full details are provided. Be sure to check it out and get involved!

What is the mint price?

We will set the MINT price individually in dollars, not in Solana. It is necessary because, as you know, the crypto market can produce fluctuations of up to 5-15% per day. 1 Nft will cost $77. (The price will be determined in SOL a few hours before the sale.) It is the amount we can use to assure our holders of the success of our following project (Phase 2).

When is the mint date?

The Enter Saiba collection mint date will be by mid-march.
