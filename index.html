About

The Project

Our Vision

Imagine a future where the NFT market is organized on a safe platform, built on a structured system free of scams, and has a unique filtering method that is loyal to the blockchain, works on the web 3.0 and helps to attract new users to the market.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a community platform, where only those teams can start building communities that have been substantially reviewed and approved by our team. We track the roadmap of each project and if the community deems it necessary we hold the creators to account for their actions.

The Beginnings

We want the Saiba Platform to start from the core of the NFT community. With this method, our team can make profits and others who will follow us along the way and see our vision. We realize this idea in 4 phases.

Phase 01

Enter Saiba

Community building

Our goal is to reach 3-5k members on the Discord server and 4-6k followers on the official Saiba Twitter page.

Launch on our Simians Universe

The project will be launched on the official website of Simians Universe.

Listing on Magic Eden

We’ll be listing the Enter Saiba collection on Solana’s best-known secondary marketplace. We provide this to give our customers the opportunity to trade early.

Phase 02

Saiba Agents

Community building

Our goal is to reach 15-20k members on the Discord server and 7-15k followers on the official Saiba Twitter page.

Launch on our Website

The project will be launched on Saiba’s official website.

Listing on ME

We’ll be listing the Enter Saiba collection on Solana’s best-known secondary marketplace. We provide this to give our customers the opportunity to trade early.

Royalty Distribution

25% of the volume trade will be allocated to certain Saiba Agents holders each month.

Staking NFT for $SAIBA Token

We provide all holders with stake opportunities for the $SAIBA Token, which will be affected by the productivity and market capitalization of the Saiba Platform.

Phase 03

Saiba Accessories

WL spots for holders in Phase 3

WL seats will be allocated among Saiba Agents holders.

Sale for only WLs

Only those will be able to mint in Phase 3, who has WL seats.

Combining Parts for Assembled Accessories

The Phase 3 collection will be a 3333 supply. We'll sell 2222 units in this collection, and 1111 will be on the market as a random airdrop. This collection will consist of 3 parts, and only those members will be eligible for the Phase 3 Utilities who assembled the individual pieces.

Royalty Distribution

30% of the volume trade will be allocated to every Fully Assembled Accessories holder each month.

Staking NFT for $SAIBA Token

We provide all holders with stake opportunities for the $SAIBA Token, which will be affected by the productivity and market capitalization of the Saiba Platform.
